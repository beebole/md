**What it does**

Localisation already works on your organization and people. With **Advanced Tags**, you can apply language, timezone and regional settings to everyone sharing a tag, in one step.

**Why use it**

- **One office, one setup** — standardise locale settings across a region in a single step.
- **Group updates** — change a whole team's localisation from one place.
- **Consistent everywhere** — keep dates, numbers and language aligned per team.
