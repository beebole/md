**What it does**

Assign work schedules to everyone sharing a tag instead of person by person.

**Why use it**

- **Roll out fast** — apply a schedule to a whole team in one action.
- **Consistent capacity** — keep working hours aligned across a group.
- **Easy to adjust** — update group schedules instantly as policies change.
