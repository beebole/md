## Public Holidays by Tag

Assign the right public-holiday calendar to everyone sharing a tag.

**Why use it**

- **Region-aware** — apply local holiday calendars to whole teams.
- **Accurate planning** — keep capacity and absence forecasts correct per location.
- **One-step updates** — change a group's holidays without editing each person.
