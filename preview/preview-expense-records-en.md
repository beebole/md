## Expenses

Capture the out-of-pocket spending your team makes on a project, right alongside the hours they log.

**Why use it**

- **Complete project cost** — combine labour and expenses to see what a project truly costs.
- **Faster reimbursements** — give finance a clear, exportable record of every claim.
- **Billable spend** — pass eligible costs straight through to your client invoices.
