## Absence Quotas

Set how much leave each person is entitled to and track what's left at a glance.

**Why use it**

- **Clear entitlements** — define yearly allowances per absence type (holiday, sick, and more).
- **Policy-ready** — carry over unused days and cap balances to your rules.
- **No over-booking** — live remaining-balance checks stop requests that exceed the quota.
