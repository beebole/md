**What it does**

Control which email and in-app notifications a tagged group receives.

**Why use it**

- **Set once, apply to many** — configure notification preferences for whole teams at once.
- **Less noise** — tailor alerts so each group only sees what's relevant.
- **Stay informed** — make sure the right people hear about the right events.

---

Available on the **Advanced** plan — or add the **Custom** add-on to your current plan.
