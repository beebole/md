## Approval Stages by Tag

Define multi-step timesheet approval flows and apply them to everyone sharing a tag.

**Why use it**

- **Multi-step review** — route approvals through several reviewers, in order.
- **Apply at scale** — set the same workflow for a whole group at once via tags.
- **Stay consistent** — keep approval rules aligned across teams and departments.
