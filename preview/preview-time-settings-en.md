**What it does**

Apply timesheet rules — like rounding, periods and entry limits — to a tagged group.

**Why use it**

- **Enforce the rules** — keep time-entry consistent across a whole team.
- **Group updates** — change settings for everyone in a group at once.
- **Fit every contract** — match policies for different offices or agreements.

Available on the **Advanced** plan — or add the **Custom** add-on to your current plan.
