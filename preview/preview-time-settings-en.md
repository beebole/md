**What it does**

Time settings already work on your organization and people. With **Advanced Tags**, you can apply the same timesheet rules — rounding, periods, entry limits — to everyone sharing a tag, in one step.

**Why use it**

- **Enforce the rules** — keep time-entry consistent across a whole team.
- **Group updates** — change settings for everyone in a group at once.
- **Fit every contract** — match policies for different offices or agreements.
