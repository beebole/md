**What it does**

Record what your team actually costs per hour to reveal the true profitability of every project.

**Why use it**

- **Internal cost rates** — set them per person, role or project.
- **Real-time margins** — see the gap between billable rates and labour cost instantly.
- **Protect profit** — spot unprofitable work early, before it eats your margin.
