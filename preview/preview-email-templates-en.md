**What it does**

Email templates already work on your organization. With **Advanced Tags**, you can tailor the emails sent to a whole tagged group, in one step.

**Why use it**

- **On-brand** — tailor wording and branding per team or client.
- **Apply at scale** — use one template across an entire group.
- **Professional** — keep every message clear, consistent and on-message.
